ABOUT US

VORTEX™ intends to deliver a simple purpose, to promote more skateboarding trends, VORTEX was formed in 2023 in Malaysia to show people about the aesthetics and the amusement of skating. Our founder, Aaron, currently a student in Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology (TARUMT) designed this webpage to remind people to go back to the old days and enjoy your life to the fullest.

Created in 2023, our company was made by a college student to sell artworks, photos of professional skaters riding skateboards and products. Although we just established the webpage, we try to make it accessible to everyone and anyone regardless.
